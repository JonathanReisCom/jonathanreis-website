Joaquina Restaurant Online Menu

https://cardapio.joaquinabar.com.br/

The idea came at that time when the population is afraid of the 'corona-virus', thinking about it, I developed the online menu integrated with the QR-Code, so that customers do not need to touch the restaurant menu and thus avoid unnecessary contacts and be a little better.
The menu was made using HTML and pure Javascript, but it was very functional and responsive as you can see by accessing the link, in addition, the QR-Code was generated in high quality so you could go to any graphics and thus be placed on the acrylic display, which was placed on each restaurant table.
